FAQ

  • Q1. The app won't launch
    Please check that your operating system is up to date.
    Reinstalling the app may also resolve the issue.

  • Q2. Currency conversion is not working
    Make sure you have an active internet connection (currency data is fetched via an online API).
    Also, check that your language settings are correctly applied.

  • Q3. How do I change the language?
    You can select your preferred language by tapping the globe icon at the top right of the app.
