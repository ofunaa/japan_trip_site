Privacy Policy

Japan Trip Go App (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Language preferences (for app localization)

  • Selected currency information (for currency conversion functionality)

  • Device type and OS version (used only for crash and performance analysis)

  • Usage analytics data (anonymous usage statistics collected via Google Analytics)

  • Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads via Google AdMob)

No personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers is collected.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide app localization based on your language preference

  • Convert currencies accurately using an external API

  • Improve app performance and stability

  • Analyze app usage patterns to enhance user experience

  • Display personalized advertisements (if you have agreed to personalized ads in your device settings)

The App does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. However, we use third-party services (Google Analytics and Google AdMob) that may collect data according to their respective privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • ExchangeRate.host for currency exchange data

  • Google Analytics for app usage analytics

  • Google AdMob for displaying advertisements

  • URL launching (e.g., for recommended apps or travel services)

These services may have their own privacy policies. Please refer to their respective websites for information on how they collect and use data.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All data remains on your device or is used temporarily for the purposes outlined above.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is no action required from you. However, if you uninstall the App, all locally stored information is automatically removed.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.
